08 Content Strategy Diagnosis

The brand likely sells food offers, not a stronger story

This section was restructured to feel more like a strategy module than a plain text block.
What feels weak

Offer-centric content profile

The visible signals suggest a feed that leans too hard on deals, platters, and transactional posts, without enough sensory storytelling or identity building.

Missing pillars

Human, process, culture, proof

Chef stories, kitchen craft, customer reactions, ambience, ingredient quality, and Egyptian cultural framing should all be stronger recurring pillars.

Recommended style

Warm, cinematic, appetite-led

Use close-ups, texture, steam, voice, Arabic cultural cues, Riyadh relevance, and stronger emotional framing to make the brand more memorable.
