06 — Snapchat

18 Million KSA Users.
Zero Presence.

No Snapchat Account Detected

Molokhia Restaurant has no confirmed Snapchat presence. With 18.21 million active users in Saudi Arabia and the platform reaching 90%+ of 18–24 year-olds, this is the most overlooked channel in this entire audit.

Why It's Non-Negotiable in KSA

  • Reaches 90%+ of 18–24 year-olds in Saudi Arabia — unmatched youth penetration
  • Saudi users spend 12h 31m/month on Snapchat — more than Instagram
  • Privacy-first architecture resonates strongly with Saudi culture and values
  • Restaurant geofilters cost SAR 50–200 and create branded UGC for free
  • Among the lowest CPMs in KSA due to lower advertiser competition
  • Disappearing Stories create urgency — ideal for daily specials and offers

Content Strategy

  • Daily specials snap: dish, price, delivery link in 10 seconds
  • Kitchen behind-the-scenes: 3× per week in authentic vertical format
  • Countdown offers: "50 meals today only" — scarcity messaging drives orders
  • Geofilter UGC reposts: reshare customer snaps using your filter to Story
  • Staff spotlight: 15-second weekly snap introducing a team member

All primary captions in Arabic. Text overlays: Arabic bold text, RTL alignment. Use Arabic or Saudi trending audio on TikTok and Reels. Avoid posting during prayer times — significantly lower engagement windows. Plan Ramadan content calendar minimum 3 weeks ahead.
