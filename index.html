06 — Snapchat

18 Million KSA Users.
Zero Presence.

No Snapchat Presence Detected

Molokhia Restaurant has no confirmed Snapchat account. With 18.21 million active Snapchat users in Saudi Arabia — the platform's highest-penetration demographic being 18–34 year-olds — this is a critical missed opportunity with low barrier to entry.

Why Snapchat is Non-Negotiable in KSA

  • Reaches 90%+ of 18–24 year-olds in Saudi Arabia
  • Saudi users spend 12h 31m/month — more than Instagram
  • Privacy-first architecture resonates with Saudi culture, especially women
  • Restaurant geofilters cost SAR 50–200 and generate free branded UGC
  • Among the lowest CPMs in KSA due to lower advertiser competition
  • Disappearing content creates urgency — ideal for daily specials

Content Strategy

  • Daily specials snap: dish, price, delivery link in 10 seconds
  • Kitchen behind-the-scenes: 3× per week, authentic vertical format
  • Countdown offers: "50 meals today only" — scarcity drives orders
  • Geofilter UGC reposts: reshare customer snaps using your filter
  • Staff spotlight: 15-second weekly snap introducing a team member

Every piece of content must map to one of five brand pillars. This ensures strategic consistency, audience variety, and measurable performance across all platforms.

🤝

Customers, reviews & UGC

Customer photo reposts, reaction videos, review highlights, fan shoutouts

🎯

Offer

Promotions & delivery CTAs

Daily specials, Ramadan deals, free delivery campaigns with direct order link

🌙

Lifestyle

Riyadh food culture fit

Molokhia at Eid, family gatherings, National Day — embeds brand in KSA occasions

Arabic-First Rules

  • All primary captions in Arabic first, English optional subtitle only
  • Text overlays on video: Arabic bold text, right-to-left alignment
  • Use Arabic or Saudi trending audio on TikTok and Reels
  • Hashtag structure: 3–4 primary Arabic tags + 3–4 secondary English tags
  • Avoid posting during prayer times — significantly lower engagement windows
  • Adjust content tone and schedule during Ramadan, Eid, and National Day
  • Plan Ramadan content calendar minimum 3 weeks in advance
  • Content should feel local — not exported international content
